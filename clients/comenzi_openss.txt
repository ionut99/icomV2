1. Generare cheie CA ( server):
	openssl genpkey -algorithm RSA -out server_key.prv -pkeyopt rsa_keygen_bits:2048 -pkeyopt rsa_keygen_pubexp:5 -aes128 -pass pass:abcd
	openssl rsa -in server_key.prv -pubout -out server_key.pub


2. Creare chei utilizatori:
	Ionut Pavel:
openssl genrsa -3 -aes-128-cbc -out ionut_pavel.prv -passout pass:abcd 2048
openssl rsa -in ionut_pavel.prv -pubout -out ionut_pavel.pub

openssl genrsa -3 -aes-128-cbc -out mihnea_vlasceanu.prv -passout pass:abcd 2048
openssl rsa -in mihnea_vlasceanu.prv -pubout -out mihnea_vlasceanu.pub

3. Creare csr pentru CA (server):
	openssl req -new -key server_key.prv -out server.csr -days 365
(creare certificat)
	openssl x509 -in server.csr -req -signkey server_key.prv -out server.cer 
4. Creare csr pentru utilizatori:
	openssl req -new -key ionut_pavel.prv -out ionut_pavel.csr -days 365
	openssl req -new -key mihnea_vlasceanu.prv -out mihnea_vlasceanu.csr -days 365

5. Semnare CSR utilzatori â‡’ creeaza certificate X.509

openssl x509 -req -in ionut_pavel.csr -out ionut_pavel.cer -CA server.cer -CAkey server_key.prv
openssl x509 -req -in mihnea_vlasceanu.csr -out mihnea_vlasceanu.cer -CA server.cer -CAkey server_key.prv
